TECHNOLOGY October 1, 2023

Harnessing technology to conserve wildlife and protect human life

Sysarks builds a powerful intelligence application for The Centre for Wildlife Studies (CWS), an internationally recognised centre of excellence in wildlife research. With the help of the application, CWS and the Karnataka Forest Department use intelligence to capture incidents and geo locations of wild animal attacks on livestock, crops and humans who reside on the fringes of the forest.
